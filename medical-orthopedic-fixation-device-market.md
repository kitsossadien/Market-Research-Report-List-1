<p><strong>The Medical Orthopedic Fixation Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Fixation Device</li><li>Internal Fixation Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The orthopedic fixation device market comprises two main types: external fixation devices and internal fixation devices. External fixation devices are applied outside the body, using rods and pins to stabilize fractures while allowing for some degree of mobility. In contrast, internal fixation devices are surgically implanted within the body, such as plates, screws, and nails, to directly stabilize broken bones. Both types aim to facilitate healing and restore functionality, catering to various orthopedic conditions and surgical needs.</p></p>
