<p><p>The anti-collision sensor market comprises four main types: radar, ultrasonic, LiDAR, and cameras. Radar utilizes radio waves to detect objects and determine their speed, making it effective in various weather conditions. Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to detect nearby objects, suitable for short-range applications. LiDAR employs laser pulses to create detailed 3D maps, offering high precision in object detection. Cameras capture visual data, enabling advanced image processing for enhanced situational awareness. Each technology serves specific needs in collision avoidance systems.</p></p>
