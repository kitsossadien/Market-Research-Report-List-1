<p><ul><li>Water Type Gravure Ink</li><li>Alcoholic Type Gravure Ink</li><li>Benzene Type Gravure Ink</li><li>Petrol Type Gravure Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gravure inks market consists of various types, each suited for specific applications. Water-type gravure inks are environmentally friendly and primarily used in packaging and publishing. Alcoholic-type gravure inks offer faster drying times and are ideal for high-speed printing. Benzene-type gravure inks, though less common due to health concerns, provide vibrant colors and excellent adhesion. Petrol-type gravure inks are highly volatile, offering quick drying but pose safety risks. Each type caters to different printing needs, balancing performance and environmental considerations.</p></p>
