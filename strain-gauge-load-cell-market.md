<p><p>The Strain Gauge Load Cell market is primarily segmented into digital and analog technology types. Digital load cells use advanced signal processing and microcontroller integration, providing higher accuracy, stability, and easy interfacing with digital systems. They often feature built-in diagnostics and can transmit data wirelessly.
