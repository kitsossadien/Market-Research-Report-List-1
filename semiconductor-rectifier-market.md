<p><p>The semiconductor rectifier market is segmented into three main types: LCR (Low Current Rectifiers), MCR (Medium Current Rectifiers), and HCR (High Current Rectifiers). LCRs are designed for low power applications, often found in signal processing. MCRs handle moderate power levels, suitable for industrial uses. HCRs are built for high-power applications, supporting heavy machinery and power conversion systems. Each type addresses specific voltage and current requirements, catering to diverse industries ranging from consumer electronics to automotive and renewable energy sectors.</p></p>
