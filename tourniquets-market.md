<p><p>The tourniquet market is segmented into three main types: buckle, manual, and electronic. Buckle tourniquets feature a simple design with a fastening mechanism for quick application, often favored by first responders. Manual tourniquets require user strength to apply pressure, providing controlled occlusion in emergencies. Electronic tourniquets use automated systems to regulate pressure, offering precise control and ease of use in critical situations. Each type caters to different needs, emphasizing efficiency and reliability in medical emergencies.</p></p>
