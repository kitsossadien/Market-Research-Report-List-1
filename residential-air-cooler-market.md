<p><p>The residential air cooler market comprises two primary types: portable air coolers and fixed air coolers. Portable air coolers are versatile and easily movable, making them suitable for various indoor spaces, providing quick and efficient cooling without installation. In contrast, fixed air coolers are installed in a specific location, such as windows or walls, offering consistent cooling for larger areas. Both types harness the principles of evaporation, making them energy-efficient alternatives to traditional air conditioning systems.</p></p>
