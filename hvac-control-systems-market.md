<p><p>The HVAC control systems market is segmented into three main types: sensors, controlled devices, and controllers. Sensors are crucial for monitoring environmental parameters like temperature, humidity, and air quality. Controlled devices, such as dampers and valves, regulate airflow and temperature based on sensor input. Controllers act as the brain of the system, processing sensor data and sending commands to controlled devices to maintain desired conditions. Together, these components ensure efficient HVAC operation and enhance comfort in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
