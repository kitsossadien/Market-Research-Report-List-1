<p><p>The manual cutting equipment market is diverse, comprising several technologies. Plasma cutting utilizes high-temperature plasma to slice through metal efficiently. Oxy-fuel cutting combines oxygen and fuel gas for cutting thicker materials. Laser cutting employs focused light beams for precision cuts, ideal for intricate shapes. Waterjet cutting uses high-pressure water mixed with abrasives for cutting hard materials without heat distortion. Carbon arc cutting employs an electric arc between carbon electrodes to melt and cut metals, catering to different industrial needs and applications.</p></p>
