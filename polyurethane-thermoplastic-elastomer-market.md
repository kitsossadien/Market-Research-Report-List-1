<p><p>The polyurethane thermoplastic elastomer (TPU) market consists of three main types: polyester, polyether, and polycaprolactone. Polyester TPU offers enhanced strength and abrasion resistance, making it suitable for automotive and industrial applications. Polyether TPU provides excellent hydrolysis resistance and versatility, ideal for medical and sporting goods. Polycaprolactone TPU features a unique balance of flexibility and durability, often used in applications requiring elasticity at low temperatures. Each type caters to specific performance needs across various industries.</p></p>
