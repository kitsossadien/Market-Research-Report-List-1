<p><ul><li>Automatic CPAP</li><li>Non-automatic CPAP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) system market is primarily divided into two types: automatic CPAP and non-automatic CPAP. Automatic CPAP adjusts the air pressure throughout the night based on detected breathing patterns, providing customized support for users with varying needs. In contrast, non-automatic CPAP delivers a fixed pressure level, which may require users to manually adjust settings if their condition changes. Both systems aim to improve sleep quality for individuals with obstructive sleep apnea, enhancing respiratory function during rest.</p></p>
