<p><p>Paddle wheel flowmeters are utilized to measure fluid flow by employing a rotating paddle that generates a flow-dependent signal. Tangential-flow paddle wheel flowmeters are designed for applications where fluid movement creates a tangential force on the paddle, enhancing accuracy in high-velocity conditions. In contrast, axis-flow paddle wheel flowmeters align the paddle parallel to the flow direction, ideal for low-velocity applications. Both types cater to diverse industrial requirements, ensuring precise flow measurements across various fluid types and operational environments.</p></p>
