<p><p>The clinical laboratory tests market encompasses various essential diagnostic panels. The Complete Blood Count (CBC) evaluates overall health and detects disorders like anemia. The Basic Metabolic Panel (BMP) measures glucose, electrolytes, and kidney function, while the Comprehensive Metabolic Panel (CMP) adds liver function tests. The Lipid Panel assesses cholesterol and triglycerides for cardiovascular risk. HbA1c tests provide long-term glucose control in diabetes management. Together, these tests are vital for disease diagnosis, monitoring, and management in clinical settings.</p></p>
