<p><p>Plastic waste management markets encompass three main types: landfill, recycling, and incineration. Landfills involve burying plastic waste, which can lead to long-term environmental issues due to potential soil and water contamination. Recycling focuses on converting plastic waste into new products, reducing the need for virgin materials and conserving resources. Incineration generates energy by burning plastic waste; however, it releases pollutants and greenhouse gases. Each method has its advantages and drawbacks, necessitating a balanced approach to plastic waste management.</p></p>
