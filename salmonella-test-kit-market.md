<p><p>The Salmonella test kit market encompasses various types, primarily focusing on PCR detection kits and rapid test kits. PCR detection kits utilize polymerase chain reaction technology to identify Salmonella DNA, offering high sensitivity and accuracy, ideal for laboratory testing. In contrast, rapid test kits provide quicker results through immunological or chromatographic methods, making them suitable for on-site or field testing. Both types are essential for ensuring food safety and public health by facilitating timely detection of Salmonella contamination.</p></p>
