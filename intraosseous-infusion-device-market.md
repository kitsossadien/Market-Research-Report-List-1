<p><p>The intraosseous infusion device market comprises three main types: battery-powered drivers, manual IO needles, and impact-driven devices. Battery-powered drivers provide consistent and controlled insertion, making procedures quicker and reducing user fatigue. Manual IO needles rely on direct hand insertion, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness but requiring more skill. Impact-driven devices utilize mechanical force to penetrate the bone, ensuring rapid access in emergency situations. Together, these devices cater to varying clinical needs and enhance emergency medical response capabilities.</p></p>
