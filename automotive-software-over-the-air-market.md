<p><p>The Automotive Software Over-The-Air (OTA) market encompasses two main connectivity types: Cellular and Wi-Fi. Cellular OTA utilizes mobile networks to facilitate software updates, ensuring coverage even in remote areas. This method is beneficial for real-time updates and telemetry. On the other hand, Wi-Fi OTA relies on local networks, providing faster download speeds and reduced data costs, ideal for urban environments or when parked. Both types enhance vehicle functionality, security, and user experience through seamless software management.</p></p>
