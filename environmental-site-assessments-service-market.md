<p><ul><li>Phase I Environmental Site Assessments Service</li><li>Phase II Environmental Site Assessments Service</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Environmental Site Assessments (ESAs) are crucial for identifying potential environmental contamination on a property. The Phase I ESA service involves reviewing historical property use, inspecting the site, and researching past records to assess risks without physical testing. In contrast, Phase II ESA service includes actual sampling and testing of soil, groundwater, and other environmental media to quantify contamination. Together, these assessments help stakeholders make informed decisions regarding property transactions, development, and compliance with environmental regulations, ensuring sustainable land use.</p></p>
