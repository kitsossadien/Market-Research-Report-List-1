<p><p>The Congestive Heart Failure (CHF) treatment devices market primarily includes Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy (CRT) and Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs). CRT devices help synchronize heartbeats by delivering electrical impulses, improving heart function and relieving symptoms. ICDs, on the other hand, monitor heart rhythms and deliver shocks if life-threatening arrhythmias occur, preventing sudden cardiac arrest. Both device types are crucial in managing CHF, enhancing the quality of life and reducing hospitalizations for patients with severe heart failure.</p></p>
