<p><p>Autocue market types can be categorized into three main types. Camera Mounted Autocues are attached directly to the camera, allowing presenters to maintain eye contact while reading the script. Floor or Stand Autocues are positioned on the floor or on a stand, ideal for larger sets where visibility can be an issue. Other market types include portable or mobile autocues, which offer versatility for various shooting environments, and software solutions that project text onto screens for digital broadcasts.</p></p>
