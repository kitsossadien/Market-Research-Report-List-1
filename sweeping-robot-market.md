<p><p>The sweeping robot market is primarily categorized into two technological types: infrared sensing technology and ultrasonic bionic technology. Infrared sensing technology utilizes infrared sensors to detect obstacles and navigate efficiently, allowing the robot to maneuver effectively around furniture and other barriers. In contrast, ultrasonic bionic technology employs ultrasonic waves to measure distances, enabling the robot to create a more precise map of its environment, enhancing cleaning efficiency. Both technologies improve the robot's operational capabilities and user convenience in maintaining clean spaces.</p></p>
