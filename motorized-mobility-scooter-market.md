<p><p>The motorized mobility scooter market is categorized mainly into Class 2 and Class 3 scooters. Class 2 scooters are designed for low-speed use, typically limited to 4mph, suitable for indoor and pavement travel. They often have a lightweight design and are ideal for short distances. In contrast, Class 3 scooters can reach speeds of up to 8mph, making them suitable for road use. They offer enhanced features like larger wheels and improved suspension, catering to users needing greater mobility across varied terrains.</p></p>
