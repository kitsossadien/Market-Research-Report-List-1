<p><strong>The Charging Surge Protectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Voltage Switch Type</li><li>Voltage Limited Type</li><li>Combination</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The charging surge protectors market can be classified into three main types: </p><p>1. **Voltage Switch Type**: This type disconnects power when voltage exceeds a certain threshold, protecting devices from high voltage spikes.</p><p>2. **Voltage Limited Type**: It limits the voltage supplied to devices, ensuring they receive only safe levels, thus preventing damage.</p><p>3. **Combination Type**: This incorporates features of both voltage switch and voltage limited types, providing comprehensive protection by disconnecting and limiting voltage simultaneously.</p></p>
